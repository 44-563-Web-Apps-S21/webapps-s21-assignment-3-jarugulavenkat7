The AntiVirus Man(A-Vira)

After COVID-19 virus attack on the world and its severe affect on the world. The world top Scientists and doctors collaborated and developed AntiVirus  Man is also called A-Vira. A-Vira detects harmful virus in the world and kills the virus before spreading. Anti-virus Man have the ability to detect the creation or birth of any kind of harmful virus in any part of the world and prevent it from spreading to the people. It has the superpower to fight with creatures trying to do harm to the human beings. The arch nemesis of  Antivirus man is  Black Jackal. Antivirus man uses his superpower to fight with the Black Jackel and group to prevent the spread of harmful virus in the world.
